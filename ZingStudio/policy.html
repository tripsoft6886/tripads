Our Privacy Pledge

Our commitment is to put users first. We strive to be transparent about how we collect and use information from our users, to keep the information we collect secure, and to provide users meaningful choices. Since our founding in 2010, Zing Studio has intentionally avoided collecting data that identifies specific individuals (such as name, email address, physical address, GPS or phone number) to the extent possible.  Zing Studio has appointed a Data Protection Officer to ensure Zing Studio maintains safe and consistent practices regarding your data and privacy.

Collection of Information

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our Services:

  • Device Identifiers.  When you interact with our Services, we do not require you to provide information that identifies you personally.  Instead, we collect and identify your device primarily using your mobile device advertising identifier, e.g. Apple’s iOS Identifier for Advertising (IDFA) or Google’s Android Advertising ID, as well as other identifiers.
  • User Names. If you would like to participate in our leaderboards, keep track of your game progress or play our multiplayer games, we may ask you to provide a username. To help protect your privacy, please do not choose a username that could be used to personally identify you, e.g. “JimSmith” or “Jsmith”.
  • Other Information You Provide to Us.  We collect the information that you provide to us, for example when you communicate with others or post, use messenger or chat services, upload or store content, contact our Technical Support team, or provide billing information.
  • Taxpayer Information. We may require players (US Citizens and Residents) to complete an online US IRS Form W-9 (Request for Taxpayer Information) if a player wins a prize in one of our online Sweepstakes. For complete rules and Sweepstakes Eligibility, please read
  • Log Information. We automatically collect information about your use of our Services and your device, including your IP Address, device model, browser version, operating system type, operating system version, mobile network information, device settings, and software data.
  • Location Information.  We derive information about your general location (city, country) from your IP Address.  Zing Studio does not collect precise location data, such as GPS coordinates, that could be used to pinpoint your physical location.
  • Information from Cookies and Other Technologies. We collect information using web beacons, cookies, and similar technologies. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve our Services and your experience, see which areas and features of our Services are popular, count visits, and understand how users interact with ads.  Web beacons are electronic images that may be used in our Services and help deliver cookies, count visits, and understand usage and campaign effectiveness. For more information about cookies and how to disable them, please see “Your Choices” below.
  • Information from Others.  We collect information about you when we receive it from other users, third parties, and affiliates. For example, when you connect your account to third-party services or sign in using a third-party partner (like Google Play Game Services, iOS Game Center or Facebook), we collect information in accordance with the privacy policies and authorization procedures determined by those services.

For example, we may process your personal data to keep track of your gameplay.  If you win one of our sweepstakes (open to residents and citizens of the US), you may be required to fill out a US IRS W-9 (Request for Taxpayer Information) so we can send you a US IRS 1099-MISC and correctly report winnings to the Internal Revenue Service.

  • Data Subject Requests. If you are a resident of the EEA, you have the right to access personal data we hold about you and to ask that your personal data be corrected, erased, or transferred. You may also have the right to withdraw a consent you previously provided, or to object to or request that we restrict certain processing. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, you may contact us as indicated below.
